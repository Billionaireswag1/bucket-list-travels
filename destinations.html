Our Top Destinations for 2026

From cultural capitals to adventure hubs, these hand-picked locations offer the perfect blend of experiences for your bucket list journey.

Kyoto, Japan — Timeless Culture and Cherry Blossoms

Why visit in 2026: Kyoto remains Japan's cultural heart. New immersive tea ceremonies, restored geisha districts, and UNESCO World Heritage sites make this the perfect year to go.

  • Cultural: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kinkaku-ji Temple
  • Adventure: Day hike to Kurama Temple, cycling through rural villages
  • Events: Aoi Matsuri Festival (May), Jidai Matsuri (October), Cherry Blossom season (March-April)

Milan, Italy — Fashion and Design Capital

Why visit in 2026: Milan's design scene is buzzing with Milan Design Week and new art exhibitions.

  • Cultural: Milan Cathedral (Duomo), Leonardo da Vinci’s The Last Supper
  • Food: Risotto alla Milanese, authentic Italian cafés
  • Events: Milan Fashion Week and Design Week

Best time to visit: April to June or September to October.

The Dolomites, Italy — Alpine Grandeur

Why visit in 2026: The Dolomites offer some of the most dramatic alpine scenery in the world.

Queenstown, New Zealand — Adventure Capital

Why visit in 2026: Adventure activities like bungy jumping and jet boating make this a must-visit destination.

  • Adventure: Skydiving, jet boating, bungy jumping
  • Nature: Lakes, mountains, and scenic drives
  • Relaxation: Wine farms and hot pools

Best time to visit: December to February for summer or June to August for skiing.

Oaxaca, Mexico — Cultural Destination

Why visit in 2026: Known for food, culture, and traditions.

  • Cultural: Monte Albán ruins and traditional markets
  • Food: Mole dishes and local street food
  • Events: Day of the Dead celebrations

Best time to visit: October to November for festivals.
